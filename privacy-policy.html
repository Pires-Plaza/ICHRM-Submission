Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the ICHRM companion app.

The app is used to provide information about the International Conference in Human Resource Management, including conference information, sessions, papers, registration access, and practical event details.

Data We Collect

The app may collect or process information needed to provide conference-related functionality, such as registration details, user preferences, selected sessions, saved papers, device information, crash logs, and basic usage data.

How We Use Data

We use this information to operate the app, provide conference information, support registration-related features, improve app stability, and maintain the conference experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data may be shared only with service providers or conference-related systems necessary to operate the app, such as hosting, registration, analytics, or crash-reporting services.

Tracking and Advertising

The app does not use third-party advertising and does not track users across other companies' apps or websites.

Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the conference service, comply with legal obligations, or support conference administration.

User Rights
